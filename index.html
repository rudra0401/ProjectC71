Capacity Building Programme
A New Initiative

A two-day enriching Capacity Building Programme on School Health and Wellness was conducted in the premises of Sarala Birla Public School, Ranchi on 15th and 16th Jan 2023, organised by CBSE COE Patna. The resource persons for the session were Mrs. Moushumi Das and Mrs. Sony Srivastava. More than 60 teachers from around 27 reputed schools of Jharkhand and Bihar attended thesession.
